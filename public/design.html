How Qorpera works
Onboard AI like you'd onboard an employee.
You don't throw a new hire into the deep end. You show them how the company works, introduce them to the team, and let them prove themselves before giving them responsibility. Qorpera works the same way.
01
2 minutes

Show it your organization

Build a simple map of your departments and team — who does what, who's responsible for what. This gives the AI the structural context no other tool provides.

02
5 minutes

Connect your tools

One-click OAuth to HubSpot, Stripe, Gmail, Google Sheets. The AI starts ingesting your data and mapping it to your organizational structure — customers to departments, invoices to teams.

03
10 minutes

Teach it what matters

Tell the AI what keeps you up at night, in plain language. "We lose track of overdue invoices." "Deals stall without anyone noticing." It learns your operational priorities and starts watching for them.

04
Ongoing

Let it earn your trust

The AI starts by showing you what it sees and recommending actions. You approve, correct, or reject. As it proves it gets things right, you let it handle more. One task type at a time. Fully reversible.

The trust gradient
AI autonomy isn't a switch. It's something earned.
Week 1

Observe

The AI watches your operations across all connected tools. It surfaces situations that need attention — with the full cross-system context to understand them.

"Invoice #4071 is 14 days overdue. This customer's email tone has shifted negative and their contract renews in 60 days."
Weeks 2–4

Propose

The AI starts recommending specific actions. You see exactly what it wants to do and why. Approve, edit, or reject — every response teaches it.

"I recommend sending a personal check-in to this customer before the payment reminder. Here's a draft based on their history."
Month 2+

Act

After 10–15 correct calls in a row, the AI suggests handling that task type on its own. You stay in control — full visibility, governance policies, instant revoke.

"You've approved all 12 of my overdue invoice follow-ups without changes. Want me to handle these automatically?"
